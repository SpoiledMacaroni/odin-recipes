Egg Fried Rice

Source: The Omnivore's Cookbook, Author: Maggie Zhu

Here's a super easy egg fried rice that you can prep and cook in 10 minutes, featuring tender eggs with crispy rice simply seasoned with scallions and salt.

Ingredients

You only need a few ingredients for egg fried rice:

  • Day-old rice
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • Optional: for extra flavor, you can add chicken bouillon or MSG

Equipment

  • a large skillet

What is day-old rice?

You might see "day-old rice", "overnight rice", or "leftover rice" for fried rice recipes. It means you should use rice that is not freshly cooked, which is hot and sticky. Overnight rice is a bit druid out, so the grains separate easily during cooking. Freshly cooked rice tends to get mushy and clump together when made into a fried rice.

Steps

To make egg fried rice:

  1. Heat the oil over medium-high heat until hot and pour in the beaten eggs.
  2. Wait until the bottom sets and scramble lightly.
  3. Add the rice when the eggs are mostly set but partially runny.
  4. Cook and stir to chop the eggs into smaller bits.
  5. Add the green onion and salt to taste, stirring to thoroughly incorporate flavors.
  6. When all ingredients are combined thoroughly, toast the rice by letting it sit without stirring.
  7. Transfer everything to a plate, and serve hot!
